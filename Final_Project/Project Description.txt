Final project: Predicting horse racing winners from the past 23 years race data

Data Source: Japan Horse Racing Association Database
Goal: Analyze the data to find out the estimated time for each horse to complete the race. The estimated time will give us the prediction of the order of placing.

Contents of Data: 
(1) Race results in the past 23 years; total time, last 600m time, ground condition, jockey, trainer, race course, race date, odds
(2) Horse Information: breed, birth date, placing by distance, placement by ground condition, placement by race course, best time by distance
(3) Jockey Information
(4) Trainer Information
